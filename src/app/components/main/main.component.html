About

We’re exploring a galaxy far far away and you’re invited!

BadHacks is IEEE NU’s annual mini-hackathon where you are not only allowed, but encouraged to bring your worst tech ideas to life. This year, we upped our marketing budget just enough to have a theme: sci-fi! Explore your dark side with the baddest hack that makes Darth Vader cringe! Challenge your inner sci-fi fan to replicate some of the futuristic tech we think is only for movies! Create something that has nothing to do with sci-fi! Whatever your counter-productive idea is, make it come to life at BadHacks.

Prizes will probably include lightsabers and up to $100 in Amazon gift cards. Of course, everyone will receive some C-3PO knowledge. The hacks will be bad… the games that we’ll have for you will probably be worse. But don’t worry, at least dinner will be good. Join us on Saturday, May 5th from 1-10PM at The Garage!

And may the 4th 5th be with you.
