About

IEEE NU is proud to host the 3rd annual BadHacks hackathon! Join us in The Garage on May 5th from 1PM to 10PM for an evening dedicated to purely useless hacks. Bring your worst sci-fi themed hack ideas and let's hack a good time! (WOW that was corny)
RSVP using this form and share the event on Facebook!

1:00 PM - 1:30 PM: Registration and Sign in

6:00 PM: Dinner

8:00 PM: Fibbage

9:00 PM: Hacking Concludes

9:00 PM - 10:30 PM: Project Expo

9:30 PM - 10:00 PM: Closing Ceremony
